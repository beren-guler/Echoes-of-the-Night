HOW TO PLAY

  • 🎵 Keep the Music Box volume high to ward off the darkness. Turn the knob — or hold W — to refill the bar.
  • 👁 When the Wardrobe Monster appears, press your flashlight once — it shines for 1 second and drives it away.
  • 💀 When something stirs under the bed, hit the pillow hard to drive it away.
  • 👂 Sometimes you'll only hear a sound — no visual. If it's a monster, hit the pillow before it ends. If it sounds like your parents, stay still.
  • ⚠️ Wrong reactions wake your parents. Two mistakes and your father arrives… game over.
  • ❤️ You have 3 hearts. Lose them all and the monster claims you forever. Survive 3 minutes to see the dawn.

KEYBOARD CONTROLS

W / ↑ Hold — increase music box volume
F Press once — flashlight (auto-off after 1 s)
Space — hit the pillow
M / B / P — spawn wardrobe / bed / sound event (debug)
